Welcome to MoveYoHead

This is a small demo project where I’m exploring WebAI concepts. I was genuinely mesmerized when I realized I could build AI-powered experiences using plain JavaScript. For a long time, I felt torn between AI and web development. I enjoyed both and could not quite choose a direction. Somehow, I do not know how, I had never heard of WebAI before. Thanks to Jason Mayes and the people who organized DevFest Istanbul, I have discovered a new passion that brings these two worlds together. I chose a VR-like, first-person experience because the AI model was already available (MediaPipe), and I wanted to finally face something I had always avoided. 3D development always intimidated me, but at the same time, I have always loved websites that use it. This project is my way of stepping into that space.
